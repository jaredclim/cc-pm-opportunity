Compensation

How you earn.

Salary plus performance-based compensation that grows as the business grows. Earnings scale meaningfully past Year 1.

Year 1 Expected Total Earnings
$60,000 – $85,000+

Range depends on production volume, gross profit margins, sales activity, and customer satisfaction. Earnings scale meaningfully as the business grows.

  • Base Salary $40,000 / year, paid bi-weekly, year-round.
  • Production Bonus Monthly bonus on revenue produced beyond what your base salary covers (~$44,400 / month). At 50%+ gross profit, you earn 7.5% on that surplus revenue.

  • Google Review Bonus $50 for every 5-star review generated on projects you manage.
